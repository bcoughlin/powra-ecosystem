## Advanced Techniques

### Animation Canceling

Animation canceling allows you to maximize your damage output by reducing downtime between abilities.

**How to execute:**
1. Use primary ability
2. Immediately follow with movement command
3. Queue next ability during animation

::: warning Skill Cap
This technique requires precise timing and practice to master.
:::

## Advanced Builds

### Glass Cannon (High Risk/High Reward)

Focus all resources on damage output:
- Prioritize attack and magic stats
- Minimal defensive investment
- Maximum burst potential

**Best for**: Skilled players who can avoid damage

### Tank Shredder

Specialized build for countering tank-heavy compositions:
- Percentage-based damage
- Armor penetration
- Sustained damage over burst
